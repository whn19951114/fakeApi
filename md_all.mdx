## Emphasis

```
*This text will be italic*
_This will also be italic_

**This text will be bold**
__This will also be bold__

_You **can** combine them_
```

_This text will be italic_
_This will also be italic_

**This text will be bold**
**This will also be bold**

## Lists

### Unordered

```
* Item 1
* Item 2
  * Item 2a
  * Item 2b

- Item 1
- Item 2
  - Item 2a
  - Item 2b

```

- Item 1
- Item 2
  - Item 2a
  - Item 2b

### Ordered

```
1. Item 1
1. Item 2
1. Item 3
  1. Item 3a
  1. Item 3b

```

1. Item 1
1. Item 2
1. Item 3
   1. Item 3a
   1. Item 3b
